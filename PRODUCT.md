# Revenant AI — Autonomous Offer Engine

Revenant AI is a fast-monetizing SaaS concept: upload your niche + product inventory, and it automatically launches and optimizes high-converting marketing assets (landing pages, ads, email sequences, scripts, and short-form content) with built-in analytics loops.

## Why this can monetize quickly

- Targets businesses with clear ROI expectations.
- Charge by generated campaigns, seats, and managed automations.
- Immediate value in first week (generated assets + launch plan).

## Core capability stack

1. **Offer Ingestion**
   - Parse existing site, docs, CRM exports, catalog CSV.
2. **Audience & Positioning Model**
   - Segment audiences and generate value-prop variants.
3. **Creative Generation**
   - Pages, ad copy, videos/scripts, social sequence.
4. **Experiment Orchestrator**
   - Multi-arm tests with budget allocation.
5. **Attribution + Optimization**
   - Pull channel metrics and rebalance winning campaigns.
6. **Monetization APIs**
   - Team workspaces, white-label exports, and agency mode.
